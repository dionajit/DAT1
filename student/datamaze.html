Datamaze

About the Event

The Department of Statistics and Data Science, Christ University, presents DATAMAZE 2023, an intra-departmental fest designed to challenge students in the fields of Data Science, Statistics, and AI. This event is an excellent opportunity for students to test their analytical skills, engage in exciting competitions, and showcase their expertise in predictive modeling and data-driven decision-making.

Event Details

📅 Date: April 19, 2023
🕐 Time: 1 PM onwards
📍 Venue: Campus View, Central Block

Why Participate?

  • Compete in challenging events related to data analysis and datamaze.
  • Gain hands-on experience in problem-solving using statistics and machine learning.
  • Collaborate with peers and enhance your analytical thinking.
  • Get recognized for your skills and expertise in data science.
