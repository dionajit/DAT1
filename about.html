About our Department

Dynamic Research and Effective Education

The Department of Data Science and Statistics strives to provide a dynamic research environment and effective education. Our curriculum integrates statistical knowledge and data science techniques, adhering to worldwide standards to address real-time, complex data challenges.

Holistic Development

We foster higher-order thinking and research skills through our curriculum and teaching pedagogy. Our co-curricular activities aim to develop essential life skills and inspire self-exploration, preparing students for the ever-evolving data industry.
